About the Great Wall

The Great Wall of China is one of the largest architectural achievements in history, built over several dynasties to protect ancient China from invasions.

  • Built from the 7th century BC to the Ming Dynasty
  • Over 21,000 kilometers long
  • Made of stone, brick, earth, and wood
