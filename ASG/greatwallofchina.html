Description

The Great Wall of China is a massive series of fortifications built across northern China to protect against invasions. It stretches thousands of kilometers and is one of the most impressive architectural achievements in human history.

History

Construction of the Great Wall began as early as the 7th century BC and continued through several dynasties, most notably the Ming Dynasty. More detailed historical information can be found on Wikipedia .
